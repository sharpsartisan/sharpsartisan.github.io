Fractal Restaurant

×

My first full project was to build a functional website from scratch, containing multiple pages and various little bits of javascript. It's much older than the other projects and I didn't know what I was doing, but really wanted to build something completely from scratch.

I wanted to imagine a website for a small business such as a cafe, but with the twist that the only items on the menu are fractals. There are also some themes of dimensional horror, and maths jokes.

I learned how to create and use modals, create an image slideshow, do a little form validation, and gain my first experience of website construction.

The site is not built with mobile devices in mind, something I want to work on for my next project.
